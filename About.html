Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and captains the Portugal national team. Often considered the best player in the world and widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or awards, four European Golden Shoes, and has been named to the UEFA Team of the Year 15 times. He has won 34 trophies in his career, including seven league titles, five UEFA Champions League titles, one UEFA European Championship, and one UEFA Nations League title. He is the first player to win league titles in England, Spain, and Italy.
